Did You Know? 'Gone Girl' Uses Voiceover to Trick You

8/18/2025, 7:00:04 PM • production


“When I think of my wife, I always think of her head. I picture cracking her lovely skull, unspooling her brains, trying to get answers.”

That’s how Gone Girl (2014) opens. Nick Dunne’s (Ben Affleck) voiceover lands like a gut punch—chilling, sharp, weirdly poetic.

And then comes Amy’s (Rosamund Pike) diary. Warm. Loving. So sincere it could be made into a Hallmark card. Her words drift in like perfume:

“I remember that moment so perfectly. The feel of his chest. The smell of his skin.” It’s soft. It’s sweet.

And it’s a trap.
