Creative Brief Template

  • Goals: business outcome, story objective
  • Audience: primary/secondary, insights
  • Message: core narrative, key proof points
  • Deliverables: formats, durations, versions
  • Channels: placements, platform notes

Shot List Blueprint

  • Priority shots: hero, product, talent, CTA
  • Coverage: wide / medium / close, cutaways
  • B‑roll: environment, transitions, texture
  • Audio: VO/script, nat sound, room tone

Storyboard Sheets

  • Structure: open → build → payoff → CTA
  • Pacing: beats, transitions, rhythm
  • Sequences: scenes, angles, motion notes

Production Checklists

  • Pre‑pro: brief, script, schedule, call sheets
  • Shoot day: gear, batteries, media, backups
  • Post: ingest, selects, edit, color, sound, export

Delivery Specs

  • File naming: project_client_YYYYMMDD_v01
  • Masters: ProRes; Deliverables: H.264/H.265
  • Aspect ratios: 16:9, 1:1, 9:16 variants
  • Platforms: bitrate, loudness, captions
