Creative Brief Template

Use the example to guide the blank template.

  • Goals: business outcome, story objective
  • Audience: primary/secondary, insights
  • Message: core narrative, key proof points
  • Deliverables: formats, durations, versions
  • Channels: placements, platform notes

Example

  • Goal: Drive demo signups for SaaS
  • Audience: Marketing managers, SMB
  • Message: Automate busywork, focus on growth
  • Deliverables: 60s brand video, 2x15s cutdowns
  • Channels: Web, LinkedIn, YouTube

Production Checklists

  • Pre‑pro: brief, script, schedule, call sheets, permits, releases, shot list, storyboards, tech scout
  • Crew + Gear: DP, AC, Sound, Gaffer, lenses, media, batteries, lights, grip
  • Logistics: call times, parking, load‑in, power, meals, safety
  • Shoot day: slate, white balance, backups on set, continuity stills
  • Post: ingest, folder structure, selects, edit, color, mix, QC, export
  • Delivery: filenames, review link, archive, invoice

Delivery Specs

  • File naming: project_client_YYYYMMDD_v01
  • Masters: ProRes; Deliverables: H.264/H.265
  • Aspect ratios: 16:9, 1:1, 9:16 variants
  • Platforms: bitrate, loudness, captions

Legal / Usage Rights (Summary)

  • Ownership: Final deliverables are licensed upon full payment.
  • Usage: Web/social/paid usage as agreed in SOW; third‑party music/licensing separate.
  • Model/Location Releases: Required for on‑camera talent and private locations.
  • Archival: We store project files for 90 days post‑delivery unless otherwise agreed.
