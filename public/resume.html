iHeart Radio · Production Partner

Storytime with the Legendary Jerry

Cochran Films handled production for this iHeart Radio podcast, capturing true stories and interviews from the golden age of Hip Hop culture—narrated by Atlanta insider Jerry Clark and built around the city’s undeniable influence on the genre.

Episode Highlight · T-Pain at Home Episode 1 features an in-home interview with T-Pain—one of many legendary conversations Cochran Films helped bring to life for iHeart Radio and Jerry Clark’s audience.

Atlanta influences everything.

The show

Storytime with the Legendary Jerry is filled with true stories and interviews from the golden age of Hip Hop culture, narrated by someone who witnessed it firsthand from Atlanta. The podcast celebrates the culture by focusing on the sensational role Atlanta has played in shaping the industry.

Each week, Jerry sits down with a guest to swap stories that shaped Hip Hop—the music, the business, the grind, and the culture. The series stands out by highlighting both Atlanta-based and worldwide creatives, with a clear through-line: the South’s impact on Hip Hop culture.
